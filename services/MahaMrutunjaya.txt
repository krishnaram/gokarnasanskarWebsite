Mruthyu means death, jaya means win over or victory. Mruthyunjaya means Win over Death.

Maha Mruthyunjaya Homam is dedicated to Lord Shiva, to avoid premature death.

This Homam is performed to achieve Jaya or Victory over Mruthyu or death, for longevity, cure for chronic illness andhave healthy life.

It helps to lessen the dangers to life due to accidents and serious ills also in removing evil effects of enemies ( Shathru Dosha Nivarana).

The negative feelings of enemies are converted to positive ones for the protection of the seekers.

Performing this homam once in year is essential to protect us and live for long life.
