Mahaganapthi is also called as Ganesha, Vinayaka, Gajanana etc.

Lord Mahaganapathi eradicates all hindrances and difficulties faced by an individual.

Mahaganapathi Homam provides a soothing effect to the mind as well as the body.

It purifies the environment as well.

Maha Ganpathi homam is recommended to a person who is suffering from losses, or when one is starting a new business or investments or one is getting married, or to remove obstacles
