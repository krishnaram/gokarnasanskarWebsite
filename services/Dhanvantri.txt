Dhanvanthri Homam is dedicated to Sri Dhanvanthari Moorthy. This homam is very useful for curing serious diseases, overcome illness.

He appears in the Vedas and Puranas as the physician of the gods (devas), and the god of Ayurvedic medicine. It is common practice in Hinduism for worshipers to pray to Dhanvantari seeking his blessings for sound health for themselves and/or others.

This homam is very powerful and has to be performed when a person is suffering from ill health and there is no option of getting well. If performed once in a year there is an atmosphere of positive energy everywhere, there by reducing ill effects on health. This homam is very useful for curing serious diseases.
