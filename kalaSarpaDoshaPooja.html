If all seven planets are in between Rahu and Ketu, it shows bad sign. That person who takes birth in this yoga may suffer from problem of instability in the life, person may be disturbed both physically and mentally, person might suffer from loss in business or there might be family problems. How To Reach Gokarna

Gokarna is well connected through roads and trains.


From Banglore to Gokarna it is about 518KM

There are multiple option via Road or Train. Several government and private buses commute daily.

  • National Highway 4, via Sirsi you can reach Gokarna.
  • National Highway 206 (NH 206/State Highway 68), via Shimogga, Honnavara you can reach Gokarna
  • One can come through KARWAR EXPRESS from Banglore City Railway Station

From Mumbai to Gokarna it is about 724KM

There are multiple option via Road or Train. Several government and private buses commute daily.

  • National Highway 04, via Belgaum and Anokla
  • National Highway 17, via Goa and Karwar
  • One can come through MATSYAGANDHA EXPRESS from Kurla Railway Station
